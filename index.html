Research & Projects
Academic Research
Household Travel Data Analysis
M.Engg Research – BUET · Accident Research Institute
Socio-economic and Mode Choice Analysis on Household Travel Data of Narayanganj City Corporation. Comprehensive transport data analysis involving descriptive statistics, feature engineering, and relationship identification across key variables. Includes OD analysis, trip generation modeling using multiple linear regression, and mode choice prediction using multinomial logistic regression and random forest techniques. Currently in preliminary stage; intended for development into a publishable master's thesis.
Big Data Project – The Simpsons
M.Sc Project · Jahangirnagar University · Supervisor: Farhana Akter Bina
Deep Analysis on the Simpsons Image Dataset. Applied machine learning and big data methodologies under the Department of Statistics and Data Science, Jahangirnagar University.
Investigating Trip Generation and Modal Choice During Emergency Dispatch in Bangladesh
Undergraduate Thesis · IUT · Supervisor: Prof. Dr. Moinul Hossain
Applied statistical and Bayesian belief techniques to analyze emergency travel demand. Explored modal choice dynamics under time-sensitive conditions and contributed to understanding transport system responsiveness in developing country contexts.
Final Year Design Project
B.Sc FYDP · Islamic University of Technology
Design of a 12-storey residential building in the Purbachal New Town Project area, the largest planned township in Bangladesh. Included Initial Environmental Examination (IEE), structural analysis and design using ETABS and AutoCAD, transportation and geotechnical considerations, construction sequencing, and overall project feasibility assessment from a multidisciplinary civil engineering perspective.
